Story

In the year 2021, within the artistic ambiance of a quaint Parisian atelier, Croisette took its first breath. Formed by Max Dubois and a tight-knit group of inventive compeers, they held a mutual desire: to revolutionize the realm of branding. Their fervent debates over coffee and intense late-night ideation sessions swiftly morphed into an innovative agency. Powered by their united goals and a passion for exceeding limits, Croisette surfaced as a vibrant player in the business, devoted to reinventing how brands resonate with their audiences and crafting narratives that engage and intrigue.

FAQs

Who do you typically work with?

We collaborate with brands, agencies, and creatives who want to push boundaries. Whether you're launching a new product or redefining a brand, we're in if you're ready to go bold.

What’s your creative process like?

We start by listening—really listening. From there, we dive into research, build concepts, storyboard, shoot, and polish. Throughout it all, we keep communication open and the vision sharp.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the scope, but most projects take anywhere from 2 to 8 weeks. We’ll always set clear timelines at the start and keep you updated at every step.

Do you handle everything in-house?

Mostly, yes. Our core team covers creative direction, production, and editing. For certain projects, we also collaborate with trusted specialists—from VFX artists to sound designers.
